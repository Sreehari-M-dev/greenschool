Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

When you log in to GPTC Green using Google, we collect your name and email address. We also collect the environmental tracking data (electricity, water, waste, trees) that you voluntarily submit through our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of the GPTC Green school project to monitor ecological footprints, aggregate school-wide data, and allow the principal to set goals.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. All data is stored securely in our MongoDB Atlas database and is accessible only to the application administrators.

4. Account Deletion

You have the option to permanently delete your account and all associated personal data directly from the profile settings within the application at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact the administrator.
